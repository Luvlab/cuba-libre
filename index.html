Libre — Cuba's Community Currency

No bank account needed. No Visa. No Mastercard. Just a free account on Cuba Libre and you're in the economy. Libre is the platform's internal exchange unit — earn it by being active, spend it on services and goods, or receive solidarity gifts from friends of Cuba worldwide.

🌱

Earn Libre

  • +100 Sign up & verify your account
  • +10 Every day you're active
  • +50 Add or verify a business listing
  • +25 Write a review
  • +100 Translate content (per string)
  • +200 Report a bug that gets fixed
  • +500 Refer a friend who joins
  • +1000 Contribute code on GitHub
🛒

Spend Libre

  • 500 List your business
  • 2000 Featured listing / month
  • 100 Post a classified ad
  • 500 Promote an event
  • any Pay for services P2P

Receive Solidarity

Friends of Cuba around the world can convert USD, EUR or any currency into Libre and send directly to you, your family, or your business. No financial system involvement.

⚡ How it works: Libre is not a cryptocurrency — no blockchain, no speculation, no volatility. It's a simple community credit stored securely in our database, backed by real community activity and solidarity. 1 Libre ≈ $0.10 USD in platform value. It cannot be cashed out to fiat, which means it operates completely outside the financial restrictions facing Cuba.
